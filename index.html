About Us Join

Come NYC It All with us.

At Stony Brook University, weekends can be pretty boring. We get it, and we’re here to help. New York City is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world, and it’s only a train’s ride away from campus. Take this opportunity to get out of the dorms, meet new people, and have memorable experiences for a ridiculously low price. Here are just some of many NYC attractions we may go see. If you're interested by any one of them, be sure to join NYC It All!

01.

Broadway

Some of the world’s best theatrical shows right in the middle of New York City! Broadway is the birthplace of such famed musicals as Hamilton and Wicked.

02.

Comic Con

Want to meet your favorite actors, YouTubers, cosplayers, and even real-life superheroes? Join us on a trip to a Comic Con NYC!

03.

High Line

Let’s see Manhattan from atop the High Line! You’ll surely enjoy walking along this 1.5-mile-long elevated track through the borough.

04.

Madame Tussaud’s

Have you every wanted to see your favorite politicians, actors, businessmen, and artists, but were afraid they’d have a horrible personality? Madame Tussaud’s is your place to be. Take pictures with wax figures of hundreds of famous people.

05.

Metropolitan Museum of Art

Interested in taking a look at some art, both ancient and modern? A trip to the Met was meant for you. NYC It All will take you to admire some of the most interesting aesthetics this world has to offer.

06.

Ripley’s Believe It or Not

Are you the kind of person who likes everything weird and wonky? Then you’ll surely enjoy Ripley’s Times Square. Come with us as we take a look at some record-breaking feats of wonder.
